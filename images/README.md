# Visualization Images

This folder contains the visualizations generated as part of the **Global Layoffs Data Visualization Dashboard** .

* **Top 10 Companies by Total Layoffs** – Displays the companies with the highest number of employees laid off.
* **Top 10 Countries by Total Layoffs** – Compares total layoffs across the most affected countries.
* **Top 10 Industries by Total Layoffs** – Highlights industries that experienced the highest workforce reductions.
* **Layoffs Trend Over Time** – Illustrates the yearly trend in global layoffs.
* **Industry Distribution** – Shows the distribution of layoff records across the top industries using a pie chart.
* **Correlation Heatmap** – Visualizes the relationships between total layoffs, layoff percentage, and funds raised.
